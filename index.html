My Story

I started Sydney Dad Guide when my first son was born.

My cousin had a boy around the same time, and whenever we planned playdates, we’d always hit the same problem… we had no idea where to go.

We’d either end up at the same local park over and over, or worse—back at one of our houses, which usually meant chaos and a massive clean-up after.

Then my second son came along… and he’s an absolute tornado. I had to start finding new places just to keep them busy and get us out of the house.

While I was searching, I noticed there were a few pages doing similar things—but mostly from a mum’s perspective. I thought, why not create something a bit different from a dad’s point of view?

I was already doing the research, already going to parks, events, and activities… so it just made sense to start sharing it.

The goal was simple—create a page I wish I had from the start.

A place where parents can jump on, find something to do, and plan ahead… instead of scrambling last minute or missing out because things are sold out.

And now, here we are.
